Adaptive Rotary Steering

Jakob Kienegger, Timo Gerkmann

This site acts as supplementary material to our work on Adaptive Rotary Steering with Joint Autoregression for Robust Extraction of Closely Moving Speakers in Dynamic Scenarios. We present target speaker tracking and enhancement examples from our synthetic, three-speaker dataset as well as recordings conducted in our acoustic lab.
